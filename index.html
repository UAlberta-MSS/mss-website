Welcome to the MSS

The Mathematical Sciences Society at the University of Alberta

About

The University of Alberta Mathematical Sciences Society provides a welcoming, safe, and inclusive space for students of all areas of study to explore their interests, pursue their passions, and collaborate with their peers. Members of the math society are encouraged to think creatively about math and explore topics outside the scope of undergraduate coursework.

Becoming a Member

The best way you can support the MSS is to become a member. Doing so will also grant you the following perks:

  • Voting role at a general meeting.
  • Ability to sign books out of the MSS Library
  • Use of the MSS printer in the lounge
  • Access to the members' channel in the MSS discord server

Cost of membership is $5 for the year. This can be paid in-person in the lounge or by e-transfer to mathmss@ualberta.ca.

Note that membership is not required to attend events or to use the lounge and library.

Events

The MSS hosts a variety of events throughout the year.

Board Game Night

Every Friday night, the MSS hosts a board game night in CAB 457 (adjacent to the lounge) from 5:00-9:00 PM. Come by to play games, hang out, and meet other students. Sometimes, pizza.

Library

The MSS oversees a small collection of textbooks and notes available for students to borrow. The library is located in the MSS lounge in CAB 453. Members can sign books out using the sign-out sheet located beside the bookshelf. Anyone is welcome to read the books in the lounge.

If you have any textbooks you would like to donate to the library, please bring them to the MSS lounge and/or contact the MSS execs at mathmss@ualberta.ca.
